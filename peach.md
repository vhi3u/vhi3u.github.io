I joined Dr. Harvey Seim's [Observational Physical Oceanography Lab](https://tarheels.live/seimlab/) in my spring semester freshman year at UNC. My project is focused on using underwater glider data from 2016-2017 to map out and analyze water mixing in the Hatteras Front off the coast of North Carolina.
